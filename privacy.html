Privacy Policy

Lava Land is a browser-based game that respects your privacy. This policy explains how the game handles your data.

Data Collection

Lava Land does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking.

Analytics & Tracking

The game does not use any analytics services, cookies, or third-party tracking scripts. No usage data is sent to any server.

Local Storage

The game may use your browser's local storage to save your settings and game progress (such as selected difficulty, theme, and current level). This data stays entirely on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Offline Play

Lava Land uses a service worker to cache game files for offline play. This caching happens entirely within your browser and involves no external servers beyond the initial download.

Third-Party Services

The game loads fonts from Google Fonts during the initial page load. Google's privacy policy applies to that request. No other third-party services are used.

Children's Privacy

Lava Land is suitable for all ages. Since no data is collected, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
